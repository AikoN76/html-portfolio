The Best Movies According to Vincenzo

My top 3 movies of all-time

The Passion of the Christ

It is a 2004 American epic biblical drama film. It depicts the Passion of Jesus largely according to the gospels of Matthew, Mark, Luke, and John. It also draws on pious accounts such as the Friday of Sorrows, along with other devotional writings, such as the reputed visions attributed to Anne Catherine Emmerich.

Interstellar

Interstellar is a 2014 epic science fiction film. Set in a dystopian future where humanity is embroiled in a catastrophic blight and famine, the film follows a group of astronauts who travel through a wormhole near Saturn in search of a new home for humankind.

About Time

About Time is a 2013 romantic science fiction comedy-drama film. The film is about a young man with the ability to time travel who tries to change his past in hopes of improving his future.
